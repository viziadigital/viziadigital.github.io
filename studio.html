How we work

01. Briefing

We start by getting to know your brand and goals, then conduct brainstorming and idea research to develop a concept that resonates with your audience.

02. Art Direction

The script is carefully developed, visual references are selected, and storyboards are created.

03. Layout

Rough animations of the future video are created using primitive objects.

04. Modeling

Detailed 3D models are created and added to the scenes.

05. Lookdev

Final lighting is adjusted, and object materials are refined.

06. Rendering and Post-Production

The video undergoes final processing, including color correction and sound design.
