Sm's food

A mouth-watering meat snack ad

This video is not recommended on an empty stomach — it’s meat time!

We were approached by a client who runs a meat snacks production company. He didn’t have a clear vision for the final product, so we analyzed current trends and suggested creating the ad in the style of "Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse". The client loved the idea, and we got to work.

In this commercial, our team focused on showcasing the product's delicious appeal. We successfully conveyed that mouth-watering effect by applying techniques we had previously mastered in our creative-educational video about Kawasaki.

The client was thrilled with the result — and now, we’re excited to finally share the video with you.
