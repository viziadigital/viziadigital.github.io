Lee Gardens Playdot

Vizualization for indoor play space

If you’re ever in Hong Kong, make sure to check out Playdot — the brand we took to the next level with a powerful 3D commercial.

This video isn’t about cute playgrounds for kids. It’s about status and vision. It’s about how investors, after watching it, will want to put their money into it.

We didn’t just list features—we built a story that gives the project a strong identity, positioning it as a leader and making the market talk about it.

Our video helped Playdot carve out its niche and leave competitors behind. It no longer relies on the media race — it stands on its own.
